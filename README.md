# Cosmic Daily ✦

A personal daily oracle web app — hosted on GitHub Pages, saved to your iPhone home screen. Enter your birthday once; open it every day for a fresh reading.

---

## What It Does

Cosmic Daily generates a deterministic daily reading based on your birth date and today's date. The same person sees the same reading every time they open the app on the same day — and a completely fresh one the next morning.

Each day you get:

- **4 category ratings** (Work & Career, Love & Social, Health & Body, Finance & Luck) — each scored 1–5 with a short message
- **Today's Oracle** — a longer daily fortune rotating between cheerful, funny, and darkly humorous tones
- **Cosmic Suggestion** — one concrete thing to do today
- **Your signs** — Western zodiac, Chinese zodiac animal, and real moon phase for today

---

## Features

- **One-time birthday entry** — stored in the browser's localStorage, never sent anywhere
- **Fully deterministic** — same reading all day, refreshes at midnight
- **Bilingual** — switch between English and 简体中文 at any time; preference is saved locally
- **Real astronomy** — moon phase calculated mathematically from the Julian date
- **iOS home screen app** — tap Share → Add to Home Screen for full-screen experience with custom icon
- **Zero dependencies** — single HTML file, works completely offline after first load
- **Luxury dark design** — frosted glass panels on a deep dark background with champagne gold accents

---

## How the Prediction Works

There is no API, no server, and no random element at runtime. The reading is generated by a seeded pseudo-random number generator (xorshift) whose seed is derived from:

```
seed = (birth_date_integer × 2654435769) XOR (today_date_integer × 2246822519)
```

Every combination of birth date × calendar date produces a unique but fully reproducible seed. The seed then drives all selections from large pre-written content pools — giving thousands of possible combinations before any repetition.

## Hosting on GitHub Pages

1. Push these files to your GitHub repo:
   - `index.html`
   - `cosmic-daily-icon.png`
   - `cosmic-daily-icon-192.png`
   - `manifest.json`

2. Go to **Settings → Pages**, set source to `main` branch, root folder

3. Save — your app is live at `https://yourusername.github.io/yourrepo/`

---

## Adding to iPhone Home Screen

1. Open the URL in **Safari** (not Chrome)
2. Tap the **Share button** at the bottom
3. Tap **Add to Home Screen**
4. Tap **Add**

The app opens full-screen with the gold ✦ icon, like a native app.

## Privacy

Only two things are stored in your browser's `localStorage`:

- `cosmic_bday` — your birth date (e.g. `1990-05-15`)
- `cosmic_lang` — your language preference (`en` or `zh`)

Nothing is ever transmitted anywhere. No network requests after first load.

---

## Technical Notes

- Moon phase uses Julian date arithmetic with a reference new moon (January 6, 2000). Accuracy ±1 day.
- Chinese zodiac uses the year-cycle method. Birth dates in Jan–Feb may be off by one animal depending on Chinese New Year timing.
- `backdrop-filter: blur()` requires Safari iOS 15+ or Chrome 76+.
